About
I'm Vince Buffalo, an aspiring evolutionary geneticist in Graham Coop's lab. I was a bioinformatician for a while, and wrote an O'Reilly book about it called Bioinformatics Data Skills. I write code and use statistics to work with genomics data and understand evolution.

My nerdy passions include plant biology, bioinformatics, statistics, and statistical computing. I also enjoy fly fishing, rock climbing, cooking, and gypsy jazz guitar.
